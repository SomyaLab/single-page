Somya Labs

Knowledge should not lose its language.

Somya builds speech and language systems for Indian languages, bolis, accents, and scripts.

Our work starts with a simple problem: much of India’s knowledge exists in forms that machines still do not understand well.

Local words, pronunciations, oral traditions, scripts, and dialects disappear when they are not recorded, searched, translated, or spoken back with care.

What we are building

We are building practical systems for speech recognition, text-to-speech, translation, OCR, voice agents, and knowledge search.

The goal is not to replace local languages with one common interface. The goal is to help knowledge move across languages without flattening the original voice.

Focus

Preserve the original. Carry the meaning. Make it usable.
